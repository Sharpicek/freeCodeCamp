Ernest Gary Gygax

Pioneer of tabletop role-playing games

Short and Lazy timeline of Garys life.

  • 1938 - Born in Chicago, Illinois
  • 1967 - Gygax co-founded the International Federation of Wargamers (IFW) with Bill Speer and Scott Duncan
  • 1977 - Release of Dungeons & Dragons Basic Set, the introductory version of D&D.
  • 1980 - Release of campaign setting Greyhawk.
